Nikon Universcale - Nikon's opto-electronics technologies allow people to explore realms beyond the range of our physical eyes. With the Nikon Universcale you can see the relative sizes of objects arranged on a single scale. This let's you grasp the sizes of things that you cannot compare side by side in the real qorld. Universcale lets you see and understand the relative size of the full range of known objects in the universe.
